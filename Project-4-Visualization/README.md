# Project 4: Data Visualization

## Objective
Build an interactive dashboard to communicate
business insights clearly to stakeholders.

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Charts, Slicers, KPI Cards)

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards: Total Orders, Total Quantity,
  Average Price, Median Price, Total Revenue
- Bar chart: Quantity by Product
- Donut chart: Quantity by Payment Method
- Column chart: Revenue by Product
- Area chart: Revenue by Month
- Slicers: Filter by Payment Method and Year

## Key Insights
- Chair generated the highest revenue at $195,620
- June was the peak revenue month at $170,616
- Online payment was the most used method
- Delivered orders accounted for 231 out of 1,200
- Premium orders (above $1,500) drove significant revenue

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $1,264,761.96
- Total Orders: 1,200
- Total Quantity: 3,535
- Average Price: $356.41
